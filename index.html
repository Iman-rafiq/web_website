University of the Punjab Gujranwala Campus

Our mission is to deliver world-class education that attracts, nurtures, and develops top talent into future leaders. We embody a culture of excellence and continuous improvement.

Established as the first teaching campus outside Lahore in 124 years, the Gujranwala Campus is strategically located near Alipur Chowk, Rawalpindi By-pass. With 37 classrooms, an administration block, a fully air-conditioned library, and a multi-purpose hall, we provide comprehensive facilities to foster an enriching educational environment.
